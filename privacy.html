Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.


Information We Collect

We may collect personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when booking appointments or using our services.


How We Use Your Information

  • To provide our grooming services.
  • To communicate with you about appointments and services.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal data. However, no online service is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.


Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as analytics providers, to improve our website. These services may collect anonymous usage data.


Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, please contact us.


Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@snazzypawsgrooming.com.
